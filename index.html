Manisha Gandhi LLM — Business & Corporate Law

Bridging Regulation
and Practice

Manisha Gandhi is an LLM graduate in Business and Corporate Law whose academic journey began in the humanities. She holds a Bachelor of Arts and a Master of Arts in English Literature, alongside a Master of Arts in Hindi — a multidisciplinary foundation that sharpened her skills in critical analysis, legal writing, and interpretive reasoning across two classical literary traditions.

Building on this humanistic grounding, Manisha pursued an LLB and subsequently an LLM in Business and Corporate Law, developing deep expertise in the regulatory architecture governing India's capital markets. Her scholarly work is driven by a conviction that rigorous legal thinking must engage meaningfully with the practical realities of securities regulation, investor protection, and corporate accountability.

She aspires to contribute to regulatory reform dialogue, particularly around SEBI's evolving mandate, IPO disclosure norms, and the governance of listed entities in India. Her diverse academic background informs a nuanced, interdisciplinary approach to legal scholarship.
